## 🧠 Behind the Honor: Growing with AIPatient

This award recognizes Huizi's outstanding oral presentation on our team's groundbreaking work published in *Communications Medicine* — **AIPatient: Simulating Patients with EHRs and LLM Powered Agentic Workflow**. As a core contributor, Huizi delivered a compelling talk on leveraging LLM-powered agentic workflows and electronic health records to build high-fidelity simulated patient systems, earning high praise from the forum judges.

- ✅ **High-Fidelity Knowledge Graph** (AIPatient KG, F1 0.89)
- ✅ **Six-Agent Reasoning RAG Architecture** achieving **94.15%** accuracy in medical QA
- ✅ **Superior Readability & Robustness**, offering new paradigms for medical education and clinical decision-making
